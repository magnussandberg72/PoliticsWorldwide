⚖️ Current Policy Proposals & Reforms

  • 1. Transparency & Temporary Lifting of Bank Secrecy (Crime Assets Recovery): Enable competent legal authorities, under independent court orders, to temporarily lift bank secrecy for targeted “from–to” transaction tracing in organized crime cases. Illegal proceeds should be seized and channelled into a dedicated Blue Light Fund to equip police, emergency medical services, and fire & rescue.

    Safeguards: judicial warrant, strict scope & time limits, audited access logs, privacy-by-design, and civilian oversight.

    / Magnus, Kalix

    “But let judgment run down as waters, and righteousness as a mighty stream.” — Amos 5:24 (KJV)
  • 2. Smart GPS Tagging for Bicycles & Light Vehicles: Require all new bicycles to be factory-equipped with a concealed, tamper-resistant GPS tag inside the frame, enabling recovery of stolen bikes and disruption of theft networks. Extend later to e-bikes, scooters and light mopeds.

    Safeguards: court-ordered access for law enforcement, encryption, minimal retention, consumer transparency, and an EU-wide technical standard.

    / Magnus, Kalix

    “For nothing is secret, that shall not be made manifest.” — Luke 8:17 (KJV)
  • 3. “Survailed Punishment” — Immediate Response Justice: Establish a dual-response framework where psychological services and police act together when crime occurs. Real-time alerts for repeat offenders and high-risk cases trigger safe detention and mandatory evaluation, followed by proportional sentencing that includes a treatment phase for serious offences.

    Goal: replace reactive punishment with proactive justice — law, psychology and mercy working together to protect the public and reform the offender.

    / Magnus, Kalix

    “For the LORD is a God of judgment: blessed are all they that wait for him.” — Isaiah 30:18 (KJV)
