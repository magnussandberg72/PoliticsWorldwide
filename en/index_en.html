✝️ Jesus Above Politics – The Way of Love

In a time when the world is divided between right and left, Jesus calls us to something greater: unity in truth and love. PoliticsWorldwide is not a party – it is a movement of hearts, for those who wish to see justice, mercy, and truth as the foundation of society.

Both right and left have something good to offer: responsibility and freedom, justice and compassion. Yet without love, both lose their purpose. That is why we say: Jesus above the blocks.

We believe that nations can be guided by love, that politics can become a service to humanity, and that the light of Christ can shine through both conservative and progressive voices – when the heart is pure and truth reigns.

This is our vision: that one day humanity will no longer speak of "right" or "left," but of God’s kingdom among us – where justice, mercy, and love rule, and Jesus holds right and left in His hands. ❤️
